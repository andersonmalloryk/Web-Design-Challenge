Cloudiness versus Latitude

This analysis reviewed temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and windspeed to allow a person to select city to travel to for vacation. In this analysis cloudiness (as a percent) was graphed against latitude to determine if parts of the world are cloudier than others. This graph suggests that latitude has little to do with cloudiness.
